our goals

Pets are big responsibility that you take until the end of their lives. Georgian reality shows that people are taking this responsibility too lightly which brings us to the main problem - stray animals.

The aim of this website is to provide information and educate people to raise thei pets in the best possible way. For people to make right decisions and also provide information on how to decrease the numbers of strays as well as how to take care of them. Stray animals are the outcome of our irresponsible behaivor in firsthand.
